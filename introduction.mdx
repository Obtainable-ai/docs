PeerNotes is a shared thinking workspace built for teams who want to capture ideas the moment they happen, develop them together, and turn them into something useful. Whether you're clipping articles from the web, dictating a thought on the go, or co-writing with a teammate in real time, PeerNotes keeps your team's thinking in one place — organised, searchable, and always moving forward.

  <Card title="Dictate a Note" icon="microphone" href="/guides/dictate-a-note">

## How it works

PeerNotes is built around a simple three-step loop that takes ideas from raw capture all the way to shared knowledge.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Capture">
    Grab ideas wherever they appear. Use the browser extension to clip a webpage, dictate a voice note on the spot, or type directly into your workspace. Nothing slips through the cracks.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Develop">
    Work on your ideas inside PeerNotes. Use the AI assistant to expand a rough thought, invite teammates to co-edit in real time, and organise notes into a structure that makes sense for your team.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share">
    When an idea is ready, share it with your team or a wider audience. Your whole workspace stays in sync so everyone always has the latest version.
  </Step>
</Steps>
