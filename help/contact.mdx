The PeerNotes team is small but responsive. Whether you've hit a bug, have a question about your account, or just want to share feedback, there's a support channel that works for you. Choose the option below that best fits your needs.

## Support channels

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Discord community" icon="discord" href="/help/contact">
    Join the PeerNotes Discord at [discord.gg/muDmrV2jq7](https://discord.gg/muDmrV2jq7) for the fastest response. Ask questions, report bugs, share feedback, and connect with other early access members. The team monitors Discord daily.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Email support" icon="envelope" href="/help/contact">
    Open a support request directly from your account settings at [peernotes.io](https://peernotes.io). Email support is best for account-specific issues, billing questions, or anything you'd prefer to keep private.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Response times during early access

<Note>
  PeerNotes is in early access with a small team. While we aim to respond to every message, Discord is the fastest route to getting help — community members are often able to answer questions too. Email responses may take one to two business days during busy periods.
</Note>

<Tip>
  Before reaching out, try searching the Help Center — chances are your question is already answered. The [FAQ](/help/faq) and [troubleshooting guide](/help/troubleshooting) cover the most common issues.
</Tip>
