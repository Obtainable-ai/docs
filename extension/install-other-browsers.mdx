---
title: "Install PeerNotes extension in Brave, Arc, or Opera"
sidebarTitle: "Other Browsers"
description: "Install the PeerNotes browser extension in Brave, Arc, or Opera by loading the unpacked extension in Developer mode — the same process as Chrome."
---

Brave, Arc, and Opera are all built on the Chromium engine and support Chrome extensions natively. This means the manual unpacked install process works the same way across all three browsers — enable Developer mode, load the unpacked folder, and you are ready to go. Follow the tab for your browser below.

<Warning>
  The PeerNotes extension is currently a pre-release build and is not yet listed in any browser extension store. This manual installation process is temporary and will be replaced by a standard one-click store install once the extension is published.
</Warning>

## Installation steps

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Brave">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Extract the ZIP file">
        Locate the downloaded PeerNotes ZIP file and extract it to a permanent folder on your computer. Brave needs continuous access to this folder, so avoid moving or deleting it after installation.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Open the Extensions page">
        Open Brave and navigate to `brave://extensions/` in the address bar.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Enable Developer mode">
        In the top-right corner of the Extensions page, toggle on **Developer mode**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Load the unpacked extension">
        Click **Load unpacked**. A file picker will open.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select the extension folder">
        Navigate to and select the folder you extracted in step 1. Click **Select Folder** (or **Open** on macOS).
      </Step>
      <Step title="Confirm the installation">
        The PeerNotes extension will appear in your Extensions list. It is now installed and ready to use.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>

## Tips

<Tip>
  **Open the extension popup.** After installing, click the PeerNotes icon in your browser toolbar to open the extension popup and sign in to your workspace.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Managing the extension.** Return to your browser's extensions page (`brave://extensions`, `arc://extensions`, or `opera://extensions`) at any time to enable, disable, or remove PeerNotes.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Updating to a newer version.** Download the latest ZIP file, extract it to replace the existing folder, then open your extensions page and click the reload icon on the PeerNotes card to apply the update.
</Tip>
