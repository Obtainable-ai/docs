## How PeerNotes works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Capture it">
    Use the web clipper to save articles and pages, or dictate your thoughts out loud. Everything lands in your workspace ready to build on — no blank page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Develop it together">
    Teammates add their judgment and context right alongside your notes. The AI assistant asks the questions your audience will ask, pressure-testing your thinking from every angle.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share it clearly">
    Publish to your shared workspace so the whole team stays on the same page. Listen to notes on a commute or catch up by reading — whichever works for you.
  </Step>
</Steps>
